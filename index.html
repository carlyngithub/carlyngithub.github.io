About

Hello! My name is Carlyn, and I'm a developer and designer, born and raised in sunny, Southern CA. I've always had a passion for functionality and design, but couldn't sit still long enough to learn how. Eventually, I entered the skilled trades to become a carpenter (which I love), and my first project was the 73-story, Wilshire Grand Hotel. After working myself out of a job, I used my free time to finally follow my passion, and learn new skills. I enrolled at Beach Coders to get my feet wet, and I've been honing my skillsets, in Front-end Development and UX Design, ever since.
